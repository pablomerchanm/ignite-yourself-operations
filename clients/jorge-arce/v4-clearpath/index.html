About

The man behind the work.

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. Medicine was not a career choice so much as a calling — a way to use his hands to give something back.

He moved to California to specialize, and never left. Over twenty-seven years he built a practice on a single, unglamorous principle: take the time the case actually needs. In 2007 that principle became the Dental Implants Center of South Bay.

Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft — still treating every patient as the person they are before the procedure they need.

— Jorge Arce, D.D.S.

How it works

Getting started doesn't have to feel clinical. The process is simple, supportive, and moves at the pace your case actually needs — from the first hello to long-term care.

01

Consultation

Start with a conversation. Your history, your concerns, your goals — heard fully before anything is proposed. You can share as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

02

Diagnosis & Plan

Imaging and careful evaluation, then a treatment pathway explained in plain language — so you understand every step before you take it.

03

Surgery

Unhurried, precise work. Twenty-seven years of steady hands, giving the case the time it actually needs.

04

Recovery

Clear guidance and close follow-up while function and comfort return — you're never left wondering what comes next.

05

Long-term care

Maintenance that protects the work — for years, not months. Because giving someone their life back includes keeping it that way.
