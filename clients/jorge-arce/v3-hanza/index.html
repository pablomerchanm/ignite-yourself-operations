01The Path

From Colombia
to the South Bay.

Dr. Arce trained in Colombia and built his life's work in California, devoting his career entirely to oral and maxillofacial surgery and dental implantology.

Board certified in implantology, he has spent twenty-seven years on the procedures most surgeons refer out — complex extractions, reconstruction, and full-arch implant cases.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay, guided by one conviction: difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back. Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft.
