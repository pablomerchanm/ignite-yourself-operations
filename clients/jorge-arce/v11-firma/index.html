The man behind the work.

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. Medicine was not a career choice so much as a calling — a way to use his hands to give something back.

He moved to California to specialize, and never left. Over twenty-seven years he built a practice on a single, unglamorous principle: take the time the case actually needs. In 2007, that principle became the Dental Implants Center of South Bay.

Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft.
