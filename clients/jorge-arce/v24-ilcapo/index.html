The production

Twenty-seven years of complex cases, shot in one take — no shortcuts, no retakes, no volume medicine.

Dr. Jorge Arce trained in Colombia and built his life's work in California, devoting his career entirely to oral and maxillofacial surgery and dental implantology. Board certified in implantology.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay — Redondo Beach and Torrance — a referral destination for the difficult extractions, reconstructions, and full-arch cases other surgeons step away from.
