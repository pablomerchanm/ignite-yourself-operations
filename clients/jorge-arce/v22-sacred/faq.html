Frequently Asked Questions

What is the practice in a nutshell? ↗
The Dental Implants Center of South Bay is the practice of Dr. Jorge Arce — a board-certified oral & maxillofacial surgeon who has spent twenty-seven years on the complex extractions, reconstructions, and full-arch implant cases other surgeons refer out. Founded in 2007, with offices in Redondo Beach and Torrance, California.
What happens at a first consultation? ↗
A long, unhurried conversation: your history, your imaging, your questions — and an honest read of what is possible, in plain language. No sales script, no pressure. You leave understanding your options, whether or not you choose to treat with us.
Am I a candidate for dental implants? ↗
Most people are — including many who were told they were not. Bone loss, failed prior work, and long-standing missing teeth complicate a case; they rarely close it. Candidacy is assessed honestly from imaging and history, never assumed.
What is full-arch (All-On-X) reconstruction? ↗
Replacing a full arch of failing or missing teeth with a fixed bridge anchored on implants. It restores function first — eating, speaking, confidence — and is planned end to end: implants, grafting where needed, sequencing, and the final restoration.
Who is behind the practice? ↗
Dr. Jorge Arce, D.D.S. — trained in Colombia, specialized in California, board certified in dental implantology. Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft.
Why do difficult cases end up here? ↗
Because the practice was built for them. The conviction since 2007: difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back. Referring dentists send the cases that need a surgeon willing to slow down.
Do you accept referrals from other dentists? ↗
Yes — referrals are the backbone of the practice. Every referred patient returns to their general dentist with a full surgical report, every time.
How is a treatment plan built? ↗
The whole case is mapped before anyone operates — implants, grafting, sequencing, restoration, maintenance. The time the case actually needs, not the time the schedule allows.
What about long-term maintenance? ↗
Restored work is protected work. Implant maintenance visits guard the investment — and the person it belongs to — for the long run.
Where are you located, and how do I reach the team? ↗
Redondo Beach and Torrance, California — serving the South Bay. Call (310) 327-4166. Visits are by appointment.
