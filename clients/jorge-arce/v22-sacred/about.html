This is careful surgery at scale

Dr. Jorge Arce is a Colombia-born, board-certified oral & maxillofacial surgeon practicing an old yet radical idea: every case deserves the time it actually needs. The founder of the Dental Implants Center of South Bay is one meticulous, stubborn craftsman. Good.

not the temperament one would normally entrust with speed — and exactly the one you want with a scalpel.

With nearly three decades of leadership in implantology and complex reconstruction, what he lacks in haste he more than makes up for with the ability to take on the extractions, rebuilds, and full-arch cases other clinicians prefer to refer elsewhere.

He believes in direct action and plain language — honest diagnosis, scalable precision, the right plan for the right patient, and long-term follow-up that protects the work for life.

The practice represents a coming together of two worlds — Colombian craft and Californian specialization.

Across twenty-seven years and two countries, one singular mission: preserve and restore the most essential function a person has — at speed and scale the case allows, never faster.

This is a practice guided by the rhythm of the person — and driven by the need to act.
