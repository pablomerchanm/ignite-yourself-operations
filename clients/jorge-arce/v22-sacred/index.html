Dr. Jorge Arce is a Colombia-born, California-forged oral & maxillofacial surgeon at the forefront of an old yet radical way to practice: unhurried surgical care. Twenty-seven years. One discipline. Every case given the time it actually needs.

not the pace one would normally expect from modern medicine — and exactly the point.

He grew up in Colombia, in a family that treated careful work as a form of respect. Medicine was not a career choice so much as a calling — a way to use his hands to give something back. He moved to California to specialize, and never left.

Board certified in implantology, he believes in direct action and plain language: honest diagnosis, meticulous planning, and surgery done once, done right. In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay.
