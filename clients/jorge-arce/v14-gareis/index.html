About

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. He trained there, moved to California to specialize, and never left. Over twenty-seven years he built a practice on a single, unglamorous principle: take the time the case actually needs.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay. Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft.
