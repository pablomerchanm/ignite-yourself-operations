About us

Dedicated to the cases others refer out

Dr. Arce trained in Colombia and built his life's work in California — twenty-seven years devoted entirely to oral and maxillofacial surgery and dental implantology. Board certified in implantology.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay, guided by one conviction: difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back.
