Commitment

Together we will build better outcomes from the ground up.

Creating
a shared impact.

We are committed to healthier, more confident communities across the South Bay. At the core of the practice is the recognition that surgical skill is not just an opportunity — it's a responsibility to create long-term positive change in the places we call home.

The Practice Pillars

Three standing commitments that shape how the practice shows up beyond the operating room.

Mentorship of Younger Surgeons +
Dr. Arce mentors the next generation of surgeons in the unhurried way — case planning, honest candidacy, and the judgment that only gets taught shoulder to shoulder.
Patient Education +
Plain-language guides and unhurried consultations, so patients decide from understanding — whether or not they treat with us.
Care That Lasts +
Long-term implant maintenance and full reports back to referring dentists — the follow-through that protects every restoration and the person it belongs to.
The Standard,
Every Day.

Held in every case, every consultation, every follow-up: difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back. That conviction founded the Center in 2007 — and it has never needed updating.

Honest Candidacy

Patients told elsewhere that nothing could be done get a real assessment — imaging, history, and options in plain language.

Referring-Dentist Reports

Every referred patient returns with a complete surgical report. Their dentist stays fully informed, every time.

The First-Consultation Guide

A free guide that tells patients what to expect — questions to ask, timelines to plan for, and what recovery really looks like.
