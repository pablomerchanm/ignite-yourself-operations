About

The man behind the work.

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. Medicine was not a career choice so much as a calling.

He moved to California to specialize and never left. Over twenty-seven years he built a practice on a single, unglamorous principle: take the time the case actually needs.

Story.

Five chapters. Two countries. One discipline, refined for nearly three decades.

Colombia — where it began

Precision was the family trade.

Before the operating room there was a household that treated careful work as a form of respect. Jorge trained as a dentist in Colombia and found the discipline that would hold his attention for a lifetime: surgery of the mouth, face, and jaw.

Medicine was a way to use his hands to give something back.

California — the specialization

He came to specialize. He never left.

California offered the depth he was looking for: advanced implantology, complex extractions, reconstruction. He devoted his career entirely to oral and maxillofacial surgery — and to the cases most clinicians prefer to send elsewhere.

Board certified in implantology, he made the difficult cases his ordinary day.

2007 — the Center

A practice built on one conviction.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay — Redondo Beach and Torrance — guided by the belief that difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back.

Not a volume clinic. A referral destination for the cases that need a surgeon willing to slow down.

The craft — six disciplines

The work others step away from.

Dental implants through full-arch All-On-X. Reconstruction where bone and tissue have been lost. Complex extractions, implant maintenance, pathology, and second opinions for patients facing hard decisions.

Not a list of services — the disciplines refined over nearly three decades.
