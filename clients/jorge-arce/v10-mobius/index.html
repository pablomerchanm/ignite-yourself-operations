Questions, answered.
Everything patients ask before their first visit — answered the way Dr. Arce answers in person: plainly.
Ask yours →
A+
What happens at a first consultation?
A long, unhurried conversation. Dr. Arce reviews your history and imaging, examines the site, and gives you an honest read of what is possible — including, when it is the right answer, the recommendation not to operate.
A+
I was told my case is impossible. Is it?
Sometimes it is — and you deserve to hear that plainly. But many of the cases in this practice arrived with that exact sentence. Complex cases are not a detour from this work; they are the work.
A+
How do I know if I'm a candidate for implants?
Bone volume, gum health, medical history, and timing all matter. The free patient guide below covers the signs — and a consultation settles it definitively with imaging.
A+
Do you work with referring dentists?
Constantly — much of this practice arrives by referral. Every referred patient goes back with a full surgical report, and the restorative work stays with your general dentist.
