About Dr. Arce

Hi, I'm Jorge.

From Colombia to the South Bay — a career spent on the cases others step away from.

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. Medicine was not a career choice so much as a calling — a way to use his hands to give something back.

He trained in Colombia and built his life's work in California, devoting his career entirely to oral and maxillofacial surgery and dental implantology. Board certified in implantology, he has spent twenty-seven years on the procedures most surgeons refer out — complex extractions, reconstruction, and full-arch implant cases.

In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay, guided by one conviction: difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back. Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft — still reading, still refining, still treating every patient as the person they are before the procedure they need.
