Where he trained

Dr. Arce trained in Colombia, then devoted his career entirely to oral & maxillofacial surgery and dental implantology.

Where he built

He built his life's work in California — the procedures most surgeons refer out: complex extractions, reconstruction, full-arch cases.

One conviction

Difficult work, done with care, gives people their lives back. In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay on it.

The work of a career spent on the difficult cases.
