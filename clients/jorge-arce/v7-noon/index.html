About
Jorge Arce — Founder, D.D.S.
He never aimed to run the biggest practice. The choice is to stay careful and independent — so that every patient works directly with the surgeon.

Jorge grew up in Colombia, the son of a family that valued precision and patience long before he ever held a scalpel. He trained there, moved to California to specialize, and never left.

This means no layers, no hand-offs, no endless back-and-forth. Just twenty-seven years of surgical judgment, clear communication, and real accountability — on complex extractions, reconstruction, and full-arch implant cases. In 2007 he founded the Dental Implants Center of South Bay on a single, unglamorous principle: take the time the case actually needs.

Outside the operating room he is a father, a mentor to younger surgeons, and a quiet student of his craft — still reading, still refining.
